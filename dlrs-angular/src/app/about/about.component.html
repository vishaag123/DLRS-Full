About Us

Diagnostic Labs is a fast growing platform for Medical Diagnostics in India. The mission is to provide affordable diagnostic solutions for a wide range of tests available for everyone. Our labs and collection centers use state-of-the-art equipments that help provide accurate test results within clinically relevant time. Our labs offer 100s of tests ranging from the simplest screening to advanced tests under molecular pathology, cytogenetics, histopathology & hematology. We also provide on-demand Sample Home collection facility.
